Ymir

Ymer or Ymir, is the oldest known creature in norse mythology. There are many tales of his origin and deeds including the one below.

Ymir video link

The story goes that the fires of Muspelheim and the ice of Jotenheim met in the void called Ginnungagap.
This meeting of 2 opposing elements caused each to react to the other, the fires of musfelheim dimmed ever so slightly
and the ice of jotenheim melted and dripped down upon the earth.

This water dripping down upon the earth gave life to the first being Ymir, who is said to be a being of life itself.
His blood can create an explosion of life where it is shed, his breath breathes life,
